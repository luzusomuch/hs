Introduction

The protection of your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. Moreover, it is important to us that you know at all times when we store data and how we use it.

The information below provides you with information on the points at which we obtain information from you during your visit to our website or your use of the app and how we handle it.

Data collection and use

Every time a user accesses a page of our offer and every time a file is retrieved, access data about this process is stored in a log file on the server.

The information in question is the following:

  • • File name of the page or file, or information conveyed by you as part of the entries (such as query parameters in the URL)
  • • File name of the page from which the current page or file was requested
  • • Date and time of the request
  • • Amount of data transmitted
  • • Access status (transmit page, page not found, etc.)
  • • Type and operating system of the web browser used
  • • IP address of the client and possibly the domain name or the name of your Internet service provider

We store this data solely to ensure system security and to improve our services.

More detailed evaluations for statistical purposes are only done anonymously. No link to personal data is established.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are sent along with the actually requested data from the Internet to your computer. There the data is stored and kept available for later retrieval.

When you visit our site, we use session cookies and persistent cookies.

The session cookie that is placed at every page request is only available during your online session and becomes inactive as soon as you close your browser software. It is only needed for technical communication between the browser and the Web server. Our cookies do not contain IP addresses.

You have the option to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies. You can adjust your browser settings, however, so that it will refuse cookies. Information on how the browser can be configured accordingly can be found at the following link: http://hilfe.telekom.de/....

If you reject cookies in general, it can lead to problems in the use of our services or you cannot use any of the other interactive features of certain web sites and services provided by cookies.

For one, we use persistent cookies to improve and to personalise our service. Moreover, the analysis tools use such cookies to make statements about recurring users. In this case, the utilisation of the data is only aggregated and never on the basis of a single user data set. It also cannot be assigned to the IP address of the client.

Of course, through your browser configuration you can ensure that cookies can be placed (refer to the explanation in the previous section). Disabling cookies, as explained in the help function of your browser, can prevent HEALTHSTARS from optimally performing its service.

In so doing, data with usage information are stored on your hard drive via a cookie (“third-party cookie”). These usage profiles are anonymous and evaluated in a technical, statistical evaluation procedure, to later enable an advertising selection related to your interests. Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google Inc. (‘Google’). Google Analytics uses so-called ‘cookies’, text files that are stored on your computer that allow an analysis of the use of the website. The information generated by the cookie about your use of this website is usually sent to a Google server in the US where it is stored. By activating the IP anonymisation on this website, your IP address from Google will be truncated within the area of the Member States of the European Union or other parties to the Agreement on the European Economic Area. Only in exceptional cases will the full IP address be transmitted to a Google server in the US and truncated there.

At the request of HEALTHSTARS, this site Google will use this information to evaluate your use of the website, to compile reports on website activity and to provide other services related to the use of the website and the Internet to HEALTHSTARS. An IP address transmitted from your browser as part of Google Analytics is not associated with other data by Google. You can prevent the use of cookies by adjusting the settings of your browser software; however, we need to point out that in this case you will not be able to fully use all the features of this site. You can also prevent the capture of data related your use of the site and generated by the cookie (including your IP address) by Google and the processing of this data by Google by following the link below and downloading and installing the available browser plug-in: http://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=de

Scalable Central Measurement Method (SZM)

HEALTHSTARS uses the “Scalable Central Measurement Method Next Generation /Skalierbare Zentrale Messverfahren next Generation” (SZMnG) of INFOnline GmbH (http://www.infonline.de) to determine the statistical characteristics for the use of our services. Measurements are collected anonymously. The SZM audience measurement uses either a cookie with the identifier “ioam.de”, or a localStorage object or a signature that is created automatically from various information transmitted by your browser. IP addresses are only processed in anonymised form.

The audience measurement was developed in compliance with data protection requirements. The goal of the audience measurement is to statistically determine user intensity and the number of users of a specific website. Individual users are not identified at any point in time. Your identity will always remain protected.

You will not receive any advertising via the system.
